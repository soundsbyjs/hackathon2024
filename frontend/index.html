Enhancing Safety and Authenticity in Online Dating

About TrueMatch

TrueMatch is an innovative application designed to make online dating safer and more authentic. By providing a secure verification platform, TrueMatch ensures the authenticity of user profiles through a comprehensive authentication process before and during in-person meetings.

How It Works

  1. Profile Verification: Users undergo a multi-factor authentication process, including a picture of their face and a form of ID, to receive a blue check mark on their dating profiles.
  2. In-Person Meeting Authentication: Before meeting in person, users can opt for an additional authentication process to ensure the person they're meeting is who they claim to be.
  3. Enjoy Safer Dating: With TrueMatch, users can have peace of mind knowing that their interactions are with verified individuals, making online dating a safer experience.
